Logos

Our logo is the core of our brand, it represents the company, our people, and our products and services.

Color

The Altus Group logo is available in 3 color-schemes: light, dark and neutral.

Sizing and spacing

Minimum size

It is important that the wordmark version of our logo remains legible at all times. The minimum acceptable size for this asset is 64px in width.

Exclusion zone

It is also important that the logo and the space around it remain clear, uncluttered and distinct from any surrounding text or other logos. You can find your exclusion zone value by taking the height of your 'A' in pixels and multiplying that by (0.66).

Usage

The following examples show things that we should never do with our logos.

Don't

use unapproved color combinations.

Don't

add drop shadows.

Don't

use on complex backgrounds.

Don't

stretch or skew.

Don't

add borders or strokes.

Don't

crop or place against the edge of a container.

Don't

edit our logo to display horizontally.

Don't

use our logo as a mask.
